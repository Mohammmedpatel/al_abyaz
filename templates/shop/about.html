Our Heritage

A Legacy of Fine Fragrances

Al-Abyaz is more than just a perfume store – it's a destination for fragrance enthusiasts who appreciate the finer things in life. Our name "Al-Abyaz" (الأبيض) means "The White" in Arabic, symbolizing purity, elegance, and sophistication.

We specialize in curating the finest collection of premium perfumes and authentic attars, sourced from renowned manufacturers and traditional artisans. Each product in our collection is carefully selected to ensure authenticity and quality.
